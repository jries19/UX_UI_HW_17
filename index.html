Projects

Utah Rivers Counsel Redesign

Overview

Utah Rivers Council is a NPO focused on protecting Utah’s waterways and water conservation.

The Problem

Users want to quickly and easily navigate the website to gather information on water conservation, and learn how they can help, but some aesthetic and usability issues limit this.

The Solution

Redesign the URC website based on user interviews and feedback from testing, to improve the overall user experience. 

My Roles

UX/UI Designer, Researcher, IA

U.S. Department of Education Redesign

Overview

U.S Department of Education is used to promote student achievement and preparation for global competitiveness by fostering educational excellence and ensuring equal access.

The Problem

Users find that navigating the Department of Education’s website can be difficult due to a lack of organization, overwhelming about of text/lack of content chunking, having to dig for information/limited navigation options, etc.

The Solution

To redesign the website with better organization, hierarchy, and improved navigation to improve usability based on user feedback and needs.

UX/UI Designer, Researcher, IA
